## Why Phrase Underperformed (Not Junk Filtering)

The 30 phrase keywords cover **100% of the search terms that converted under broad**. Every converting search term from the broad period would be matched by at least one current phrase keyword.

The issue is auction volume, not coverage:

| Source | Conv/day | Impressions |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| `pllc` broad alone | 0.63 | 8,409 |
| All other broad keywords | 0.22 | 3,251 |
| All 30 phrase keywords | 0.45 | 4,651 |

The 30 phrase keywords actually outperform the non-`pllc` broad keywords (0.45 vs 0.22 conv/day). But they can't replicate what `pllc` broad did — Google's algorithm bids more aggressively into auctions with one broad keyword on a short, high-volume term than with 30 specific phrase keywords covering the same ground.
